About Us

Our Mission

Jesus Power International Evangelical Ministry is dedicated to spreading the message of Jesus Christ, promoting spiritual growth, and serving our community with love and compassion. We believe in the transformative power of faith and the importance of building a strong, supportive church community.

Our Values

  • Faith: We trust in God's word and guidance
  • Love: We serve with compassion and kindness
  • Community: We build strong bonds through fellowship
  • Service: We give back to those in need
